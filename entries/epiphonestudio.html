2005 Epiphone Les Paul Studio

Guitar Specifications/History

Guitar Specifications/History

This guitar is stock 2005 Les Paul Studio; old catalogs indicate the guitar has standard Epiphone AlNiCo pickups and hardware, like the
Grover tuners that come on this guitar. However, it does have an aftermarket SKB case. It's serial number dates it to 2004, but inside
the case lies the usermanual, as well as the hangtags from the store of purchase... dated 02/03/05.

Technical Details

  • Mahogany Body & Neck
  • Maple Top
  • Rosewood Fingerboard
  • Cherry Sunburst Finish
  • Stock Pickups

Personal History

I don't know how exactly it got to where it is but this is another guitar courtesy of grandparents. I guess this was just part of the larger
collection and passed down. From there, it sat for a while until I began to play guitar again. At this time there was only the other Epiphone and
this guitar... and I went towards this one instead of the other Epiphone and played this guitar for a while until I got my Fender.
Afterwards this guitar was left to rot in its case until a few months ago, when I brought this guitar back from the dead, cleaned it up, and brought
it back into rotation. Afterwards, however, I found it's really bad about breaking strings. Without a doubt this is the guitar that's broken the most strings for me.
Having played my Fender for so long, this guitar is definitely a different tonal character than that guitar. As a result, I tend to use it for deeper, darker
sounds that the snappier sounding Fender is unable to acheive easily.
