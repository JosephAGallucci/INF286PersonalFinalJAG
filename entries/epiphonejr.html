2015 Epiphone Les Paul Junior

Guitar Specifications/History

I can't seem to figure out the exact origin of this specific model. I wanna say it came in a starter pack but I don't remember this ever coming in a starter pack
combined with the fact that had it been a pack, I would have had an Epiphone amp and not a Line 6. Perhaps it was some store bundle? Hard to say at this point.
Epiphone catalogs never listed this model but you can find old archives of people purchasing them off the rack. Stock it had a humbucker and cheaper tuners.

Technical Details

  • Mahogany Body & Neck
  • Rosewood Fingerboard
  • Sunburst Finish
  • Seymour Duncan Phat Cat Pickup

Personal History

Where it all began for me. This had to have been some kind of gift because I do not remember ever asking for a guitar. Regardless, I recieved it From
my grandfather who was an avid guitar fan. For the rest of the vacation I got this on and for a little while longer, I learned the basics of guitar on this...
until middle school came up, where I dropped it in favor of trumpet and didn't play guitar for years afterwards. When I did pick the instrument back up, I still
left this guitar abandoned in favor of other guitars/basses.
For my 19th birthday, I got new Kluson tuners and a Seymour Duncan Phat Cat, which is a P90 pickup in a humbucker case. This guitar then went
on the workbench and upgraded with said parts... before I snapped a string on the bridge a day later. I haven't really touched it since mostly
because I'd want to replace the bridge completely and my project ideas right now are targeted towards a different project.
