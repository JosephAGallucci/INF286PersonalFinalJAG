2022 G&L Fullerton Deluxe LB100

Guitar Specifications/History

While the specifications sheet for this particular bass lists the instrument as being built in 2022, in fact it was purchased the following year. This color seems to be
one of the last of its kind, based on it's serial number, and was purchased as a B-stock model. However, the only issues listed were mild and did not affect the instrument that much. In contrast, the store
had another B stock LB-100, the very same model as this bass but with a different color, with notable finish damage. This bass only has a small finish issue that does not affect the playability.

Technical Details

  • Alder Body
  • Maple Neck & Fingerboard
  • Shoreline Gold Finish
  • LB-100 Stock Pickup

Personal History

I had been looking for a nicer bass as a graduation gift, and spent many months over my last year of high school looking over different models and figuring out what I wanted.
By April, I had solidifed on the type of bass that I wanted - a Precision-style bass, yet this particular model snuck up on me. I had been out for what was at first just a normal vetting
session, where I tried out a few models to see what I liked... I walked out of Sweetwater that day with a bass in tow, not expecting to have one chosen out. From all of my bass collection,
this is my personal favorite for it's playability and sound.
