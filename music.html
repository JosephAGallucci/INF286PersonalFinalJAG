Music I've Done

Below are two major pieces of music that I've written over the past few years

A Mole is a Unit

This particular piece is not an original, but is rather a cover of a piece called "A Mole is a Unit", done in a different style to the original. I made this back in 2022 after hearing the original song in chemistry class. I had this impulse idea "what if I made a cover of this song."

...I'm still half wondering if that was a good idea.

Nevertheless, I learned that song, created this arrangement for it, and played all of the instruments on it.

This particular arrangement is a re-record that I did the following year.

Going Up!

My most recent, currently unfinished work. This song spurred from an impulse thought at a Norse Code Radio meeting.

"What if there was a song that sampled the talking elevators in Griffin Hall?"

I had been starved for songwriting, so I took the idea and wrote the basic chords and feel of the song. Then, I worked with another guitar player and trombone player to record and create the final version of this song. Myself, I played bass and produced the track.
